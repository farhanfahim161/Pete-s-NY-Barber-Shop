About Us

The New York Barber Experience

Pete's New York Barber has been a cornerstone of men's grooming in the city for over two decades. What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into one of NYC's most trusted barbershops.

Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves a premium experience. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll feel the difference. Our skilled barbers take the time to understand your style and deliver a cut that exceeds expectations.
